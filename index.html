AI Keyboard – mobile app & custom keyboard

Privacy Policy & Data Sharing Rules

Last updated: 20 March 2025

1. Who we are

AI Keyboard (“we”, “us”, “our service”) respects user privacy and complies with Apple App Review Guideline 5.1.2(i), GDPR, and other applicable regulations.

Privacy contact: ridiks100@gmail.com.

2. What data we process

  • User text for AI. Only after you give explicit consent do we send selected text to the third-party AI provider OpenRouter (https://openrouter.ai) to generate responses. We do not store these texts on our own servers.
  • Keyboard preferences and prompts. Settings, saved prompts, or snippets are synced through App Group / iCloud solely across your devices.
  • MiniTanks multiplayer data. For online matches we transmit the PIN, match events, tank coordinates, and system toasts to our WebSocket server at wss://ws.aikeyboard.pp.ua. We do not collect personal conversations, and we discourage sending private content via in-game messages.
  • Performance analytics. Anonymous crash reports and diagnostics may be processed by Apple/Google services to improve stability.

3. How we obtain your consent

  • During onboarding the app shows a clear explanation of how data is sent and links to this document. Checking the “I agree” box is required before AI features are enabled.
  • You can revoke consent at any time in settings (“Settings → Privacy → AI Processing”). After revocation we prevent any AI text transfer.
  • MiniTanks multiplayer asks for a separate consent before connecting to the WebSocket server.

4. Use and sharing of data

We use the data strictly for:

  • Generating AI responses via OpenRouter upon your request;
  • Syncing prompts/snippets between your devices;
  • Routing MiniTanks multiplayer sessions through our WebSocket server;
  • Analyzing crashes and fixing bugs.

We do not sell personal data or share it with advertising networks. Third-party processors (OpenRouter, Apple, Google) only receive data required for their function and operate under their own privacy policies.

5. Your rights and control

  • Revoke AI or multiplayer consent in the app/system settings whenever you wish.
  • Delete stored prompts, PIN history, or other local items manually.
  • Contact ridiks100@gmail.com to request a data copy, correction, or deletion (subject to applicable laws).

6. Security

We rely on TLS/WSS encryption, App Group sandboxing, and strict API access controls. No method is 100% foolproof, so please avoid entering passwords, payment data, or sensitive secrets into AI prompts.

7. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Policy updates

We periodically audit third-party services and update this policy whenever data-sharing practices change. New versions take effect once published.
